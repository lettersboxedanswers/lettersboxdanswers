What Is the Letter Boxed Puzzle?

Letter Boxed is a popular daily word puzzle by The New York Times. The game presents players with twelve letters arranged around the edges of a square. The objective is to create valid English words that:

  • Use all twelve letters at least once
  • Contain a minimum of three letters per word
  • Avoid using consecutive letters from the same side of the square
  • Connect words so the last letter of one word becomes the first letter of the next

Because of these constraints, even experienced word-game players can struggle to find correct solutions consistently.
